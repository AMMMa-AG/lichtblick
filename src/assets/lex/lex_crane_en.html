crane (crane shot): A special device capable of lifting and moving the camera through the air. A crane resembles a huge metallic arm and may be either stationary or moveable. In a crane shot, the camera (often together with the cinematographer) is above ground level and mobile. This allows it to dynamically change the framing and quickly reveal a very large space. In contrast to a dolly shot, the crane has the full freedom of both horizontal and vertical movement.
