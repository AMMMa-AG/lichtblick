available light: In most cases, the lighting arrangement is oriented towards the “natural light” sources in the setting, such as street lamps, general daylight or other lamps and lights which are positioned within it. All of these sources are important for creating a realistic or non-realistic atmosphere, and depending on the context of the setting, may have little or significant relevance for the character’s or subject’s lighting. For related information, see the entries concerning exterior light, diegetic and non diegetic.
