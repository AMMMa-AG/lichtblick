ambient sound: A diegetic sound or collection of sounds apparently coming from within the film’s world in contrast to an exterior layer of sound produced from outside of it, e.g. sound effects or a film score. In particular, the term “ambient sound” refers to the background sounds which are normally present in a certain scene or location. Common ambient sounds include wind, water, birds, crowds, office noises, traffic noises, etc.

Ambient sound has a number of functions including:
  • providing audio continuity between shots,
  • avoiding an unnatural silence in a film,
  • establishing and supporting the mood of a scene.
