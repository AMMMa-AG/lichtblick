anamorphic lens: A special camera lens that squeezes and distorts a widescreen image onto standard film material. In the cinema, another corresponding lens on the projector unsqueezes the image to its normal shape and to a ratio of 2.35:1 (Cinemascope). Ultra-Panavision 70 with an aspect ratio of 2.76:1 is an example of another anamorphic format using special lenses on the camera and the projector.
