Shapes: Shapes within the frame are a very important tool for filmmakers in order to direct the viewer's attention and to achieve a certain compositional setting. The effect shapes can have on the viewer depends on the context of the shot, in particular, and the context of the film, in general. Nevertheless, shapes typically have a strong impact on the viewer's experience or emotional response to a shot. For this reason, you can generally assume that their appearance in a frame is not by coincidence.
