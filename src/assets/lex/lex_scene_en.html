scene: A series of interrelated shots in a narrative film evoking the impression of continuous action that usually takes place in a single time and place, often with the same characters. Sometimes a single scene may contain two lines of action occurring in different places or even different times that are related by means of crosscutting. Several interrelated scenes make up sequences that do not obey the spatial and temporal unities of a single scene. A whole scene which is rendered in one single shot is called a sequence shot (or plan scene, plan-s√©quence). The end of a scene is often indicated by a change in time, action and/or location.
