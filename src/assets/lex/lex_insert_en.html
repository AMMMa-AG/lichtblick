insert (shot): A shot of part of a scene as filmed from a different position, camera distance, camera angle or height and/or focal length from the master shot. Inserts cover action already covered in the master shot, but emphasize a different aspect of that action due to the different framing. This kind of shot points out some important detail in a scene that must be given the camera's full attention for a moment. An insert is different from a cutaway in that the cutaway is of action not covered in the master shot.
