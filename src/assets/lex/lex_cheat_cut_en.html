cheat cut: A cheat cut is a transition between two shots, in which the second shot is filmed from a camera position that should actually be impossible given the positions of the figures and objects from the previous shot, which thus leads to a mismatch. For example, a cheat cut might switch to a camera position, where in the previous shot it seemed there was a solid wall. Obviously, cheat cuts can be more easily implemented when the filming takes place at a studio set instead of outdoor locations, since in studios, supposedly solid objects like walls can easily be moved out of the way.
