point of view: Different modes of narration are possible when telling the story of a film:

  • First person narrator: A character tells the story from his or her perspective.
  • Third-person omniscient narrator: The narrator is not involved in the story and has no limitation of knowledge.
  • Third-person limited narrator: As above; however, in this case, the narrator’s knowledge is limited.

These different modes of narration in film can be effectively implemented through the use of appropriate camera techniques in order to achieve the corresponding narrative effects. Such techniques include the objective camera, the subjective camera, the omniscient camera and the point-of-view shot.
