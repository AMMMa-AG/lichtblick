fisheye lens: An extreme wide-angle lens that is capable of showing an angle of view close to 180°. Shots taken with a fisheye lens are always considerably distorted, thus producing images that appear obviously artificial. In some films, fisheye lenses (or reflective decoration elements and props that produce a similar effect) are part of the mise en scène, e.g. door peepholes, curved mirrors, etc.
