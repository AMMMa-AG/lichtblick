realism: In filmmaking, a certain style which favours the subject matter as content over aesthetic form. Realist filmmakers are interested in what is current or real. They try to present things and conditions as they really are (at least in their world view) and strive to reproduce or closely recreate reality. Realistic films often have documentary-style aspects. In film, realism is often associated with social realism and kitchen sink realism.
