rack focus: Rack focus or racking focus refers to the practice of changing the focus of a lens during a shot in such a way that an element in one plane of the image goes out of focus and an element on another plane in the image comes into focus, thus making a visual connection between those elements. In many cases, rack focus suggests a kind of cause-effect relationship.
