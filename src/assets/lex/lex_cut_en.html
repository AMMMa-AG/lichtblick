cut: A cut marks the connection between two shots and merges the end of one shot with the beginning of the following shot. In most cases, this is done by a so-called “straight cut” which means that the last frame of the first shot is instantly followed by the first frame of the next shot. With a straight cut, no special optical transition is used. As a verb, to cut means 1. the action of implementing a cut and 2. to change from one shot to another apparently instantaneously.
