genre (film): A category label used to classify fictional film based on a loose set of defining features or stylistic criteria. Over time, genres come to be associated with certain conventions that nevertheless change continually as new genres are developed and older ones fall out of use. For this reason, genre is always a vague term with no fixed boundaries, and often, a film fits into multiple genres.
Some common film genres are action films, westerns, science-fiction films, thrillers, horror films, gangster films, musicals.
